About Us

Saraswathy Flour and Rice Mill, established in 1999, has been a cornerstone of quality in Piravom. With over two decades of experience, we specialize in a variety of products, including Kashmiri chili powder, coriander powder, sambar powder, malasala powder, and fresh coconut oil.

Our commitment to excellence ensures that all our products are meticulously washed and dried in-house, preserving their natural flavors and freshness. We take pride in using traditional methods combined with modern technology to deliver spices and oils that enhance the culinary experience. Our team is dedicated to sourcing high-quality ingredients, ensuring that every product meets the highest standards of purity and taste.

At Saraswathy, we believe in quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction, making us a trusted choice for households and businesses alike. As we continue to grow, we remain focused on innovation and sustainability, aiming to bring the rich flavors of our products to kitchens everywhere while maintaining our dedication to the environment and our community.
